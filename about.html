About World of Discipline

A Spirit-filled church committed to raising disciplined, devoted followers of Jesus Christ who live out the Word daily.

15+ Years of Ministry

Who We Are

Built on the Word,
moved by the Spirit

World of Discipline (WOD) is a vibrant, Spirit-filled church community in Accra, Ghana. We are passionate about biblical truth, disciplined Christian living, and authentic worship. Our desire is to see believers grow into mature disciples who impact their families, workplaces, and society for the glory of God.

  • ✦
    Our Mission

    To make fully devoted followers of Christ who are disciplined in prayer, grounded in Scripture, and active in service.

  • ◎
    Our Vision

    To see a generation of believers walking in God's purpose — disciplined, anointed, and world-changing.

  • ◈
    Our Values

    Integrity • Excellence • Community • Discipleship • Faith in the power of the Gospel

Become Part of Our Family

Our Journey

From Humble Beginnings to a Growing Family

Founded in 2010, World of Discipline started as a small home fellowship with a burning desire to see lives transformed through the teaching of God’s Word and the power of the Holy Spirit. Today, we are a thriving church community with vibrant ministries, passionate worship, and a heart for outreach.

Our Core Values

Integrity
