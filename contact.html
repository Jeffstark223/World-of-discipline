Questions We Often Hear

What should I wear to a WOD service? +
Come as you are! We want you to feel comfortable. Whether you dress casually or formally, you are welcome. What matters most is that you come with an open heart ready to encounter God.
What time should I arrive for my first visit? +
We recommend arriving 10–15 minutes before the service begins. Our ushers will be happy to welcome you, answer questions, and help you find a good seat. Sunday services start at 7:00 AM and 9:30 AM.
Is there parking available at the church? +
Yes, we have on-site parking available for all service times. Our parking team will be on hand to assist you. We also encourage carpooling and the use of ride-sharing services like Bolt and Uber.
Are children welcome during services? +
Absolutely! We have a dedicated Children's Church that runs simultaneously with the main service. Your children will enjoy age-appropriate Bible lessons, worship, and activities in a safe, loving environment.
How can I get baptised or become a member? +
We hold baptism and membership classes regularly. Fill out the contact form above selecting "Baptism / Membership" and our team will reach out to schedule you into the next available class.
How do I join a ministry or cell group? +
We have ministries for all ages and gifts — worship, prayer, outreach, youth, and more. Speak with any of our ushers after a service or send us a message through the contact form. We'd love to connect you with the right team.
