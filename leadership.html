What Guides Us

Leadership Principles

🙏
Servant Leadership

We lead not to be served, but to serve. Every decision is made with the congregation's spiritual growth and wellbeing at heart.

📖
Word-Centred

Scripture is our foundation and final authority. Our leadership is anchored in the truth and wisdom of God's Word.

🤝
Accountable & Transparent

We hold ourselves to the highest standard of integrity — accountable to God, to each other, and to the people we shepherd.
