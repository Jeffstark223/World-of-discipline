Our Ministries

Every believer has a gift and a place to serve. Discover where God is calling you to make an impact.

🙏
Prayer Ministry

The heartbeat of WOD. We intercede for our church, city, nation, and the nations through disciplined, fervent prayer.

🎵
Worship & Arts

Leading the church into God’s presence through anointed music, dance, drama, and creative expressions of worship.

⚡
WOD Youth

Raising a generation of young people who are on fire for God, grounded in Scripture, and walking in purpose and discipline.

🌍
Outreach & Missions

Taking the love of Christ beyond the church walls — feeding the hungry, clothing the needy, and preaching the Gospel.

👶
Children's Ministry

Building a strong foundation of faith in our children through fun, interactive Bible teaching and discipleship.

🌿
Women of Virtue

Empowering women to walk in biblical womanhood, spiritual maturity, purpose, and sisterhood.

Your Place to Serve

Find Your Ministry

Every gift, every talent, every passion has a place at WOD. Whether you sing, pray, teach, serve, or give — there is a ministry waiting for you. Here's how to get connected.

Connect With Us
1
Visit a Sunday Service

Come as you are. Every Sunday at 7:00 AM or 9:30 AM is a great time to experience WOD for the first time.

2
Speak to a Pastor

After service, our pastors and ministry leads are available to answer questions and help you discern where you fit.

3
Join a Ministry Team

Step in, serve faithfully, and watch God use your gifts to transform lives — starting with your own.
